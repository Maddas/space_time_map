Introduction

The project is an online map called spatial temporal map on Giardiasis events in buffalo. Basically, the map displays spatial-temporal event points of Giardiasis in city of buffalo between 2013 to 2017. You can click the "start" button at the bottom of the page to see the event points changed by the year (represent by ), so that you can see the trend of happening of Giardiasis in terms of time interval. Moreover, you can choose the one year to see the distribution of in that year. Finally, the "Return" button can send you to the main personal website.
